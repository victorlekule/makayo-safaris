Connecting the Coast to the Wilderness

Tanzania is home to some of Africa’s most legendary national parks and landscapes. Morix Beyond Zanzibar seamlessly connects your Zanzibar beach holiday with unforgettable mainland adventures.

From vast savannahs and volcanic craters to towering mountains, we create experiences that blend adventure, comfort, and authentic exploration.

Iconic Destinations

Serengeti National Park

World-famous for endless plains and the Great Migration. Extraordinary game drives with lions, cheetahs, and vast herds.

Ngorongoro National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site. Spot the Big Five in a single day within a stunning volcanic crater.

Tarangire National Park

Renowned for giant baobab trees and large elephant populations. Ideal for short safaris from Zanzibar.

Lake Manyara National Park

Famous for tree-climbing lions, flamingos, and lush forests. Perfect for diverse landscapes and birdlife.

Mikumi National Park

Highly accessible with wide open savannahs and abundant wildlife. Excellent for first-time safari guests.

Ruhaa National Park

Tanzania's largest national park, covering over 20,000 km² in the country's southern circuit, renowned for its remote, untouched wilderness and massive elephant population.
