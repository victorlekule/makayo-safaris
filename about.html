ORIGINS

Born from the
African Dust

Makayo Safaris wasn't created in a boardroom; it was forged around campfires under the vast Tanzanian sky. What started as a singular passion for the untamed wilderness has evolved into a premier luxury travel atelier.

We are local experts, conservationists, and storytellers. For over 15 years, we have guided discerning travelers through the Great Migration, to the summit of Kilimanjaro, and across the azure waters of Zanzibar.

Makayo Team

Our Mission

To deliver transformative, tailor-made African experiences that deeply connect our guests to the wildlife, landscapes, and cultures of Tanzania, while fiercely protecting the ecosystems we operate within.

Our Vision

To be the undisputed pinnacle of luxury and sustainable travel in East Africa, setting the global standard for how premium tourism can uplift local communities and preserve the natural world for generations to come.
